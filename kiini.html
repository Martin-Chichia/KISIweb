Who We Are

Kiini sustainable initiative (KISI) is a local Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) established and registered in December 2007 under the section 10 of the NGO Coordinating Act of Kenya Government. Mandated to work in Four counties namely Nyeri, Kirinyaga, Laikipia and Muranga counties.

It composes of a pool of experts with expansive knowledge in rural agriculture, community development, community livelihoods, community governance, and community health and community sanitation sectors.

Our Vision

A self-sustainable and resourceful local community.

Our Mission

To provide a platform for the underprivileged and marginalized people through mobilization, sensitization and utilization of the hidden resources, in order to meet their socio-economic needs in Kenya
